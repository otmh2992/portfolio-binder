## 🎯 Key UX Moments

### **1. The Wait (Timer)**
- Builds anticipation
- Encourages spontaneous choice
- "Quick decision" psychology

### **2. The Click**
- Immediate visual feedback
- Everything else fades away
- Focus on username creation

### **3. The Roll (Animation)**
- Slot machine excitement
- Progressive reveal (left→right)
- 1.5 seconds of engagement

### **4. The Lock**
- Clear visual confirmation
- No ambiguity about what happened
- "This is yours now" feeling

### **5. The Choice**
- Option to change mind
- Low-pressure decision
- But encourages commitment

## 🎨 Design Notes

### **Colors:**
- Primary: Blue (#0073e6) - Trust, creativity
- Success: Green - Confirmation
- Muted: Grey - Supporting info
- Foreground: Black/White - Readability

### **Typography:**
- Headings: Montserrat (Bold, modern)
- Body: Inter (Clean, readable)
- Username: Monospace (Technical, unique)

### **Spacing:**
- Generous padding (p-8)
- Clear sections
- Visual breathing room
